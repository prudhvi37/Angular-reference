A directive is responsible for modifying template.

There are three types are directives in Angular.

Attribute Directives,Structural Directives and Components. 

Components are internally directives with a template.

Attribute directives changes the appearance or behaviour of an element or another directive.

Structural directives—change the DOM layout by adding and removing DOM elements.
